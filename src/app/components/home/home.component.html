About Veggie.com "Veggie is a unique online marketplace that enables farmers to sell directly to customers without the intervention of middlemen".

Veggie.com was established in 2019 with a sole goal to curb farmer suicides in developing countries. In order to achieve this goal, we strive to provide a fair price for the farmer’s produce by giving them a platform to sell directly to their customers. This way farmers get to keep most of the profit and consumers can get a good price and get to know the farmer who grew the produce.

Our Farmers Neha, joined us 2015 "A farmer is a magician who produces money from the mud".

Neha is a thirty-five-year-old farmer producing tomatoes and onions. She has been a farmer since she was fifteen. She has a family of five which includes her husband and three of her kids who help her in farming. She joined Veggie group of farmers to get a fair price and transparency. She also enjoys interacting with the consumers.
OUR CONSUMERS Anika, joined in 2016 "In today's day, it is very difficult to find farm fresh vegetables. Supermarkets do sell organic produce but what is the guarantee that they actually are fresh?".

Anika is a college professor and a home-maker for a family of four. She is responsible for the nutrition of the family. She likes to ensure that she buys the best and freshest produce available to her at an affordable price.
